Questions

Everything you're wondering.

Do I need coding experience to join?
Not at all. Code With Her is built to be beginner friendly — mentors guide you through every step, and many participants write their first real project on the day.
Can I come without a team?
Absolutely. Come solo and we'll help you find your people during the morning team-forming session. Teams are 2–4 people.
Is Catalyst really women only?
Yes. Code With Her is a women-only space by design — a place to build confidence and community without hesitation. Allies can support us as mentors, sponsors, and partners.
What should I bring?
Just your laptop, charger, and curiosity. Food, drinks, workspace, mentorship, and good energy are all on us.
How much does it cost to attend?
Registration is free for participants — Catalyst is made possible by our partners so that opportunity stays open to everyone.
