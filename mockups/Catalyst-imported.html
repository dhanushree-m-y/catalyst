Why Catalyst

One opportunity can change everything.

Catalyst was built by someone who once found her own opportunity through a hackathon — and saw how a single open door can redirect a whole career.

Today, Catalyst exists to create more of those doors, for every woman ready to begin.

A room where you don't have to prove you belong — because everyone already knows you do.

How the day flows

Eight hours, start to celebration.

Hour 0

Check-in & kickoff

Arrive, grab coffee, and settle in. A warm welcome and a quick tour of the day.

Hour 1

Team up & ideate

Find your team of 2–4, or get matched on the spot. Pick a problem worth solving.

Hours 2–6

Build with mentors

The core sprint. Workshops run alongside, and mentors circulate to help you ship.

Hour 7

Demos & feedback

Show what you made. Friendly judges give real, kind, useful feedback.

Hour 8

Awards & celebration

Prizes, certificates, and a group photo. You leave a builder — and part of Catalyst.

Questions

Good to know.

Do I need to know how to code?
Not at all. Code With Her is beginner-friendly by design. Mentors and workshops meet you exactly where you are — whether it's your first line of code or your fiftieth project.
Who can join Catalyst?
Any woman curious about technology — students, recent graduates, and career-switchers alike. Come solo or with friends; we'll help you find a team of 2–4.
How much does it cost?
Participation is free, funded by our partners. That includes meals, mentorship, workshops, and everything you need for the day.
What should I bring?
A laptop, your charger, and an open mind. We'll take care of the food, the space, the mentors, and the good energy.
Will I actually build something?
Yes. The day is structured so every team leaves with a working first version to demo — and the confidence that you can build the next one too.
